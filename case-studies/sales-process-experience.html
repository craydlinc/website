The Challenge

A 35-year-old remodeling firm with 25 Google five-star ratings faced sales friction. Clients hesitated committing to major additions without price certainty, and the all-or-nothing design contract created trust barriers.

Despite decades of experience and a stellar reputation, the traditional sales process wasn’t showcasing the firm’s capabilities. Homeowners were left to imagine results from 2D drawings—leading to uncertainty, delayed decisions, and lost opportunities.

The Solution: A Two-Step Virtual-First On-Ramp

CRAYDL designed a phased engagement process that removed friction and built confidence at every step.

Phase What the Client Gets The Benefit
Phase 1: Preliminary Pricing LiDAR scan, high-level floor plans & elevations, budget accuracy from 20% refined to 10% via budget.craydl.com “Go/No-Go” decision point at minimal investment
Phase 2: Full Virtual Build Integrated 3D model (engineering, interior design, landscaping), VR walkthrough, permitting & approvals included, every element quantified Fixed-price contracts with zero on-site surprises

The Results

Highest-Revenue Month in 30-Year Company History

  • Zero on-site surprises—projects were pre-built and priced virtually
  • $3.2 million total bookings across 4 projects (3 additions, 1 remodel)
  • 80% win ratio with a 59-day average sales cycle
