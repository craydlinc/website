The Project

CRAYDL serves builders, developers, and homeowners requiring precision in custom luxury residential construction. By designing the home once virtually, and producing documentation and artifacts from it for the field, CRAYDL de-risks and speeds up projects with deep industry expertise and technology.

This was a high-end luxury remodel. The homeowners—retired with construction experience—understood the risks of building without complete plans. Their interior designer, however, had provided no detailed drawings. CRAYDL performed a complete home scan to capture every detail, then integrated the interior design specifications into the model for confident decision-making before any field work began.

The CRAYDL investment: $18,000. The prevented rework value: $168,000.

The “5-Inch” Miracle

The designer’s plans included 5 inches of non-existent space. Custom cabinetry would have failed to fit on-site. The virtual model flagged the discrepancy before construction, preventing $20,000 in tear-out and re-ordering fees.

Eliminating “Homeowner’s Regret”

2D blueprints failed to communicate the fireplace design intent. Upon walking through the 3D model, the homeowners realized:

  • Stone facing was incomplete—it didn’t wrap the walls
  • The mantle appeared undersized for the space scale
  • The design didn’t match their functional expectations

The design was redesigned virtually in real-time. Minimum $15,000 prevented in masonry demolition and rebuild costs.

By The Numbers

114 critical design changes were identified through the virtual model—each one a potential field conflict, budget surprise, or homeowner disappointment caught before a single nail was driven.
