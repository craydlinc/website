The 7-step plan

Your Roadmap to a Predictable Project

Seven phases from feasibility through permitting—aligned to how luxury jobs actually run.

  1. Feasibility: Budget, scope, and timing grounded before design accelerates.
  2. The Digital Twin: Precise 3D model including structural and mechanical systems.
  3. Architectural Development: Plans, massing, roof, assemblies—validated in BIM.
  4. Interior Design: Finishes in the model with options and cost visibility.
  5. Landscape Design: Hardscape and exterior materials coordinated with architecture.
  6. Engineering Coordination: Virtual stress-test; clashes resolved before permit and field.
  7. Permitting: Build-ready package. Permit support available.
