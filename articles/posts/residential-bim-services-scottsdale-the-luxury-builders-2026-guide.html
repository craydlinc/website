The Anatomy of a Clash

Clashes come in two distinct flavors: hard and soft. A hard clash occurs when two objects, like a recessed lighting fixture and a massive structural header, try to exist in the same physical space. Soft clashes represent clearance issues where a water heater lacks the 30-inch service access required by code. Luxury Scottsdale homes often feature complex, hidden HVAC systems and integrated smart technology that compete for space in tight plenum areas. Virtual Design and Construction (VDC) manages these high-density zones with lightning-fast accuracy, ensuring every wire and duct has a dedicated path.
