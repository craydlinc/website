In the world of homebuilding and remodeling, preconstruction processes and profitability often weigh heavily on professionals. Adam Katz, the driving force behind Craydl, has transformed the industry with his groundbreaking platform and services for preconstruction management.

Adam’s unique perspective combines financial acumen and hands-on construction experience gained during his 12-year stint on Wall Street and his passion for home redevelopment. He later transitioned to IT consulting and software development, becoming VP of Operations at ImageTag and streamlining complex processes, a skill that became central to Craydl’s mission.

Bridging the Gap in Construction Management

Craydl was created to address the lack of a unified platform for information management and services to support the preconstruction process. Adam drew on his experience in development, building, and IT consulting to develop a solution that streamlines communication and collaboration within the industry. The platform revolves around a “single version of the truth,” providing a centralized repository for all construction documentation and bidding processes.

Passionate Problem Solving and Impact

Adam’s passion for problem-solving led him to create meaningful change in the construction industry by building a team of like-minded professionals dedicated to dismantling dysfunctional construction processes.

With a deep-rooted commitment to drive change and Adam’s leadership, the Cradyl team has delivered tangible improvements, including a 10% margin gain on each construction project. Builders, architects, and other stakeholders within the construction “village” benefit from better communication, enhanced execution, margin optimization, and increased happiness.

Life Beyond Craydl: Family, Passion, and Learning

Adam Katz and his family

Adam is a devoted husband and father who loves that next challenge. When he’s not working or hanging out with his family, he can often be found on the ice, gliding effortlessly across the rink as he plays hockey with his friends. As he looks to the future, Adam envisions a symphony of success that harmonizes his passions with his unwavering dedication to revolutionizing the construction industry.
