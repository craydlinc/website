Frequently asked questions

CRAYDL FAQs

What does CRAYDL do?

CRAYDL is a builder-first Virtual Design and Construction (VDC) partner that helps prevent costly rework by making the invisible, visible. Nearly 90% of construction rework can be traced back to failures in planning. CRAYDL eliminates these failures by aligning all stakeholders early and identifying mistakes, omissions, and clashes before construction begins.

What problems does CRAYDL help solve?

CRAYDL exists to eliminate busts in the field caused by: constructability issues (inaccurate existing conditions; discrepancies between architectural sheets and details; conflicts across structural, MEP, and architectural designs); homeowner challenges (difficulty reading plans and understanding design intent; misaligned or incomplete interior design documentation); and bad takeoffs (inaccurate, missing, or conflicting information; frequent design or scope changes). In short, CRAYDL brings clarity, coordination, and confidence to every project phase.

How does CRAYDL accomplish this?

1. Solving constructability issues: Iterative clash detection, fully coordinated BIM, and high-density LiDAR scanning (¼ inch accuracy).
2. Helping homeowners visualize: Interior design construction documents from the model; model and render selections so clients can see rather than read plans.
3. Eliminating bad takeoffs: Model updates automatically update sheets and quantities; quantities generated directly from the model.
4. Building a true BIM: Quantify materials accurately; detect clashes, gaps, and inconsistencies; create immersive VR experiences.

Who does CRAYDL serve?

CRAYDL is builder-first, partnering primarily with builders. We also support homeowners, interior designers, architects, and developers seeking a more coordinated, transparent, and predictable project delivery experience.

Does CRAYDL provide architectural or engineering services?

CRAYDL can provide architectural design support and collaborates with licensed architectural partners when licensure is required. We manage and coordinate engineering services but rely on qualified third-party engineers to perform the work.

Will CRAYDL help me obtain permits?

Yes. CRAYDL provides comprehensive management for city and Planning & Development approvals, HOA submittals, and engineering coordination. We manage the entire submittal process so you can stay focused on design and construction.

How long does the process take?

Timeline depends on project type. We use clear, phase-based flows so you know what to expect.

New home (full design through approvals): Typically 6–16 months. Phases include: Feasibility (architectural control, site & zoning, budget, program set-up) → Existing conditions (scan, BIM of existing) → Architectural development (concept through construction documents) → Landscape integration → Interior design integration → Engineering (civil, SMEP, energy code, utilities) → Approvals (HOA, hillside, planning & development).

Remodel: Phases are tailored to your scope (existing conditions, design development, interior/landscape integration, engineering, and approvals as needed).
